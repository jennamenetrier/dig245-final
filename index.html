Click me to learn more about what logos mean! Designing a good logo is more than just art. 80% of people buy a brand based on the color scheme of the logo. Brands are important because they make your product unique and authentic. Consumers are also more likely to buy from a brand they trust and they like. If you create a logo that is memorable, unique, and attractive to the consumer then you are more likely to sell more of your products. Nike is a great example. They are unique and memorable and they have a strong reputation which promotes sales across the world. In fact, 95% of people in the United States are aware of the brand Nike. Promoting brand awareness through your logo is a great way to increase your sales of your product. Nike started with a simple logo that they paid someone to make for $35. Years later that logo is now worth over $30 billion. The history of the Nike logo shows how a simple design, along with cosumers trust in the brand, marketing, etc. can turn into a multi billion dollar net worth. A logo is the backbone of a company and a poor logo can result in a decrease in consumer confidence in the product and ultimately a decrease in sales. So, next time you create a logo keep in mind that sometimes a simple design with an asthetically pleasing color scheme can help build brand awareness.
